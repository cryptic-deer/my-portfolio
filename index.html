Hello there.

Here you'll find a showcase of some of my work. I'm a new developer and I'm primarily self taught in this discipline, but I take time to research, tinker, and learn more every day.

I discovered my passion for Web Developement and Web Design while attending classes in University. However my courses didn't satisfy my curiosity, so I started to learn independently and realized how broad is the world of a developer.

Before starting University, I worked in factories and welded in my Father's small business. My first encounter with a programming language was in High-school, at the time I was aspiring to be a Mechanical Engineer so I stuided PLC and AutoCAD. Later I realized that for me, working in an industrial environment was really limiting and I couldn't use my ability to make something creative and unique.

I feel my strength lies not just in writing code or designing UI, but in solving problems. I pride myself in my ability to listen, learn, and determine what folks truly need. Providing solutions at every step of the way, from concept to execution.

Currently I'm learning ReactJS and TailwindCSS to bring my skills up to date with recent standards, but I started to take interest in Python too. If you think I may be a good fit for your team, please feel free to reach out. Let's build something great together!
